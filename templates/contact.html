Frequently Asked Questions

How do I submit a complaint?
You can submit a complaint by clicking on the "Submit a Complaint" link on the homepage and filling out the form.
How do I track my complaint?
You can track your complaint by clicking on the "Track Your Complaint" link on the homepage and entering your complaint ID.
How long does it take to resolve a complaint?
The time it takes to resolve a complaint depends on the nature of the complaint and the complexity of the issue. We strive to resolve complaints as quickly and efficiently as possible.
